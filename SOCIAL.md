## 💼 LinkedIn Post

**Headline: Beyond Keywords: Searching the Visual World with Sound 🎙️➡️📺**

I’m excited to share my latest project: **WhisperGrid**.

In the traditional search paradigm, we are limited by the words we can type. But human expression is much richer than text. We hum, we whistle, we describe "vibes" that words often fail to capture.

WhisperGrid is a multimodal retrieval showcase that bridges the gap between audio and video. 

**How it works:**
Using the new **Gemini Embedding 2** model, the app maps both audio and video into a shared semantic space. When you record a sound, the app calculates the mathematical "closeness" (Cosine Similarity) between your audio and a library of videos, highlighting the best match in real-time.

**Key Technical Highlights:**
✅ **Multimodal Vectors**: Native support for audio-to-video retrieval.
✅ **Real-time Feedback**: Canvas-based audio visualizer for live recording feedback.
✅ **Quantitative Matching**: See the exact cosine similarity score for every match.
✅ **Cinematic UI**: Built with React and Framer Motion for a premium, responsive experience.

This project demonstrates the power of the Gemini API in creating intuitive, human-centric interfaces.
